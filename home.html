PayMe

The Solution To Track All Invoices

Previous Next

VIEW GITHUB

PayMe is an invoicing solution that was created to enable companies the ability to keep track of paid, outstanding, and past due expenses of their clients. Companies that register to the site are able to perform CRUD functionality, adding and deleting clients, creating, updating, and deleting invoices, as well as a print page feature of invoices.

Companies can create a login for their client and provide them the login credentials. Clients who log in to the page has a separate dashboard and are able to view all invoices related to their company.
